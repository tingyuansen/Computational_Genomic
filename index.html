Workshop

Rationale

We invite researchers and students working in computational genomics or related fields to participate in a one-day hybrid workshop on the 31st August 2023 on ANU Campus.

The workshop will discuss latest advances and challenges in these interdisciplines at the interface between Computing, Biology, and Medical research, and other cognate disciplines. Topics may include (but not limited to) methods, models and applications of bioinformatics and computational biology for genomic data. We will bring together local and international students and researchers, in particular, those who worked with Dr. Yu Lin before. The idea of holding this workshop is inspired by the memory and intellectual legacy of Dr Yu Lin, whose seminal contributions and collaborative spirit are having a lasting influence in the research field and the ANU community.

The workshop will consist of long and short presentations in the morning, followed by break-out sessions and a panel dialogue on emerging trends and challenges in the afternoon.
